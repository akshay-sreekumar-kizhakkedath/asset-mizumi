Sustainability

Where conscious living comes naturally

At Mizumi Reserve, sustainability isn’t an afterthought - it’s at the core of our design. From renewable energy to smart engineering, every detail minimises our footprint. A mindful way of building, for a future that’s shared with the planet.

Sponge Effect

Designed to utilise every drop of rainwater.

Smart water systems reduce your utility bills while keeping your home future-ready. It’s comfort, savings, and sustainability - built in.

Smart Power

Total utilisation of renewable energy sources.

Here, power is generated from solar panels. In addition, the project is designed in accordance with the sun’s natural path to maximise natural light for less dependency  on the artificial.

Zero Out

Zero waste to landfill.

1200 kg of organic waste is converted to manure in the organic waste converter.

Climate Capsule

Higher green cover  for lower temperatures.

With more green cover,  Mizumi Reserve has a considerably lower temperature than the city beyond its walls.
